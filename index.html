My name is Fernando Palacios, and I specialize in the production of visual projects, from start to finish.

In the past, I've done team direction for IN THE PALACE International Short Film Festival, self-produced and edited a digital magazine of interviews to global creatives, co-produced two environmental documentaries with my own video production company, worked as a head barista in super-luxury restaurants, and explored different projects of photo-manipulation and art direction, travelling photography and poetry, cover art and graphic design, and new media manipulation.
