This project is an exploration from Studio Anxl on the possibilities of online publishing.

The current edition seeks to explore the possibilites of combining audio recordings with a fairly direct, unfiltered editorial transcription.

The concept followed the idea of collecting a variety of voices, presenting them -as is- (with minimal editorial alterations).

The resulting style of the texts may take an unpolished appearance, as with any prototype, for the concept wanted to preserve the idioms of talk.

The author is greatly thankful to all of the featured interviewees on this prototype, as well as to those who did not leave the cutting floor.
