bóreas.

Role: Editor & Editorial Designer
Production, InDesign, Printing

Bóreas was my first project of physical printing, with a limited print run focused around a number of my hometown's best creative profiles.

The final books were distributed amonst the capital's best design & production companies, in order to make myself known, and get a foot in the door.

The whole project was self-produced, from the editorial design and the producion of the interviews, to the direction of photography for the final printing process.
