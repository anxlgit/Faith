Shortviews

Role: Project Direction

Year: 2018

Shortviews encompasses +40 video interviews and +80 text interviews in different languages, but also the direction of four different teams of multidisciplinary volunteers.

I travelled to Bulgaria to join In The Palace, an international short film festival, as a designer, producer, and project director for the festival's media presence.

Here I learned the importance of structure, and the ways in which crisis can be dealt with, but also the importance of clarity and sincerity under situations of stress.
