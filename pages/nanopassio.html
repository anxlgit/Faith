nanopassio.

Role: Editorial Design
Year: 2016

I've always been a sucker for conceptual photography, but so far, not so good at photographing stuff. And I've always loved to write.

In this project, I decided to turn both passions into a single object: a magazine on conceptual spaces.

For the first time using editorial design tools, this came out as a really interesting experiment, and it taught me the importance of order and scale.
