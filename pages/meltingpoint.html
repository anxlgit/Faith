The Melting Point.

Role: Management & Co-Production
Year: 2020 to 2021

This was a documentary centered around a few teams of climate scientists, racing down to gather all possible data from the last glaciers in the Pyrenees before they melt down.

It was selected in multiple film festivals all over the world, in spite of the pandemic, and set for broadcast on a major regional TV channel.

I was in charge of the co-production and the management of the project's goals and schedule.
