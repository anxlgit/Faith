The Melting Point.

Role: Co-Production & Management
Year: 2020 & 2021

This was a documentary centered around a few teams of climate scientists, racing down to gather all possible data from the last glaciers in the Pyrenees before they melt down.

The final piece was awarded by the PRISMAS, Spain's most prestigious science communications prize, selected in multiple film festivals all over the world, and set for broadcast on a major regional TV channel.
