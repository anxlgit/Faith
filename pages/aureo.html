Role: Producer & Art Director
Production, Photography, Branding

The main goal of this project was to carry out the artistic and conceptual direction of a fictitious product, and leverage the resulting photographs to design a fitting brand identity.

Reversing the usual process, I first defined the heart of the identity (environmentally responsible jewelry) and then expanded that concept through photography and art (painted natural scenes in metallic colors).

Finally, I expanded this concept into its own identity, with typographic and visual elements supporting the brand's unique values.
