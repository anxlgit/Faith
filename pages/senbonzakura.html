Rol: Art Director & Photographer
Production, Photography, Coloring

The main goal of this project was to direct a product photography session applied to a fictional jewelry brand.

The production process focused on finding models with different yet compatible skin tones, whose hands would emphasize the interplay of rings against flowery, colourful backgrounds.

Ultimately, I sought a final coloring result that respected the natural tones of the skin while emphasizing the aesthetic and importance of the rings, the main product.
