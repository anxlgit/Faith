bodyes and visions.

Role: Concept Artist & Designer
Photography, Manipulation, Photoshop

These are some of my proudest pieces from when I started practicing graphic design: photographies of the self, combined with some mangled first steps into desing software.

Their purpose was to play, and to learn the ways in which image manipulation and recombination could be used to draw meaning from separate elements.

The process taught me the basics of photography and scale, as well as the ins-and-outs of color correction, gradients and masking techniques.
