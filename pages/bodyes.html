bodyes and visions.

Role: Visual Art

Year: 2016

These are some of my proudest pieces: failures in flesh, all depictions of ways in which hopes and strains afflict us.

Their purpose was to play, and to learn the ways in which image manipulation and recombination could be used to draw meaning.

The process taught me the basics of photography and scale, as well as the ins-and-outs of color correction and masking techniques.
