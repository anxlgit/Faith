Role: Art Installation
Year: 2017

In 2017, I was selected to take part in Etopia's residence/academy for the development of new media art on its giant “Media Façade”.

During three weeks, we were coached by some of the best in new media art and visual coding, in order to develop our own art pieces.

Mine and a few others were selected to join a recap with best pieces from past editions, to be re-scheduled for additional screenings.
