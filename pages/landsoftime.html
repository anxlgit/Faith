lands of time.

Role: Photography
Year: 2019

Seas and stone can function as archives; and here they do, of a past so remote that even books have forgotten its stories.

This was my introduction to photography, whereas before I had only been in charge of managing those who took and shot the pictures and videos.

Although my equipment was very limited, I managed to do with what I had, and learned to use the contrast of lights and shadows to my advantage.

As with all other projects, the voyage was self-funded. Only adventure leads to growth.
